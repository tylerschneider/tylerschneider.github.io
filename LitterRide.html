Litter Ride

Litter Ride is an excersize app created for the company Discovelo. The app is intended to be used along with an exercise bike in order to promote excersize, regulate and track emotions of childen at home or schools. I worked on this project for about a month with a group of five people.

Contributions

  • 2D Artist - Created art for the menu screen and UI Icons
  • 3D Artist - Modeled and textured the player character, clothing items, and environment objects
  • UI - Designed and developed the UI for the player customization menu
  • Unity Development - Added functionality to the player customization including changing items on the character, colors of the items, and shop/buying mechanics

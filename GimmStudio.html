GIMM Studio

Quick Summary

Environment • Modeling • Lighting • Texturing • Graphic Design

GIMM Studio is an multiplayer VR game created by everyone in the GIMM program at Boise State University with the purpose of providing a virtual online classroom and place to showcase student work. My primary role on this project was as an environment artist and I worked over the course of one semester. The environment was used for the GIMM 2020 Senior Showcase.

Contributions

  • 3D Artist - Worked off of concept art to create 3D models and textures for the environment including the arcade interior's textures, tables, chairs,
  • Lighting - Helped with lighting inside the arcade
  • Layout - Contributed to laying out the arcade interior
  • Logo - Created the logo for GIMM Studio

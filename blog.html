Oculus Rift S vs Valve Index

Oct 26 2020

 Consumer virtual reality devices are a relatively new thing, but as time goes on, more and more of these devices are released. Out of all of these devices, there are two that I have tried: the Oculus Rift S and the Valve Index. Both of these devices have their own pros and cons, but is one objectively better than the other? Is the Index worth over double the price? Spoilers, the answer is yes, but not necessarily because of the technology itself.


 I first started my VR adventures with the Oculus Rift S. Like any VR device, it can be used for a variety of entertainment and experiences, usually for games or social interactions. This is also the first, and so far only device, I have tried developing for. The headset works extremely well, with great controller tracking despite the lack of any kind of camera or external sensors. As for the display, it looks decent and gets the job done, but can use definite improvement as the technology advances.
